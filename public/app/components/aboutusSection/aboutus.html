What is Novowash?

Novowash aims to build India's first organized chain providing end-to-end home interior care solutions to urban households, who need premium service quality as well as ease and convenience in their busy life-style. We look to build not just a brand but an eco-system of partners who would be standardized by our strategic technology partnerships, highly trained professionals and complete digitized service deliverance. Novowash is committed to working with entrepreneurs and investors to co-build Novowash brand through the franchise route. We have built a team of passionate individuals looking to build a sustainable business in the direct-to-home services segment and look to become an overall segment creator.

Our founders

While working together in their previous company our founders looked deeply into the problems consumers faced when dealing with home and car care service providers. There was a huge need for creating not just a market-place but an eco-system of organized and standardized chain of service providers. Novowash was built with great effort and understanding of the current market and requirement for high quality hassle free services.
